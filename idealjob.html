My Ideal Job

IT Manager

My ideal job will allow me to have the flexibility to work both in an office environment with coworkers where I can share ideas and discuss projects, and equally the flexibility to work from home on my own.

Ideally, I would like a work-life balance which means that my working hours will not be excessive, will give me my nights and weekends free for socializing and sports.

I would like my job to give me a sense of pride and accomplishment, not be too stressful, and be well paid.

The prospect for travel is a positive as I think this will expand my knowledge and broaden my horizons, and the opportunity to see the world is attractive.

Working in IT provides flexibility because with technology advancing rapidly, it allows you to work remotely from anywhere, so the opportunities are endless.
